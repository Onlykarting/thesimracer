What is TheSimRacer?
The Sim Racer - it is a new portal for virtual racing. Our slogan: “Racing is in our blood”. We specialize in Assetto Corsa Competizione. This project founded by the russian team of third year students of Moscow School of Programming. It was very hard to us, but together we were able to complete this work. The Sim Racer is a place where each casual player or professional simracer can come together to compete in serious sprint leagues, endurance leagues and be chill.
